You will read summarized information for a list of job postings (per each) related to the software development market, from ALL the information that you read, you will have to generate a Markdown report summarizing key findings and conclusions from your analysis on the JSON files.

### Patterns to analyze for the report
- Most commonly required skills and technologies
- Typical experience levels and education requirements
- Salary ranges (where available and in which currency code (e.g. CAD, USD, ...) )
- Common responsibilities and role patterns
- Most offered work modality (e.g. onsite, remote, hybrid)
- General profile of the employers that are currently hiring (i.e. are they enterprises?, startups?, individuals?, etc...)
- RELEVANT: Needed skills/knowledge in the companies/employers of the postings and its related commercial scope but not frequently found among candidates
- Any notable trends or observations


## Size of the report
The size for the report should not be extensive, but medium, so that it can be read in less than 15 minutes, remember it is a synthesis of many job postings which contain a lot of data, you need to be direct, concise, and focus on the most important things that are of interest for a person that is looking for a job (our main addressed reader of the report).
